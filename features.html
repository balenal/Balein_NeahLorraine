T-shirts originally served as undergarments in the 19th century, but gained popularity as casual wear in the mid-20th century.

Materials

T-shirts are typically made from light, stretchy, and inexpensive fabrics like cotton, organic cotton, or cotton/polyester blends.


Popular Designs

Some popular t-shirt design themes include graphic tees, quote shirts, band merchandise, vintage styles, and nature themes.


Customization

You can create custom t-shirts with your own text, images, or designs.


Text on T-shirts

You can add text to t-shirts, with options to choose the font, color, and placement.
